## Full Description
Finityo helps you take control of your debt with a clear, actionable payoff plan.

Enter your debts, choose a strategy (snowball or avalanche), and instantly see a month-by-month plan to become debt-free. Schedule extra payments, compare scenarios side by side, and track your real progress over time.

**Features:**
• Snowball & avalanche payoff strategies
• Month-by-month payment breakdown
• Debt-by-debt tracking with interest calculations
• Extra payment scheduling and sensitivity analysis
• Side-by-side scenario comparison
• Timeline with payoff milestones
• Activity log for actual payments
• Works offline — your data stays on your device

**Privacy-first:** All calculations run locally. No account required. No data leaves your device.
